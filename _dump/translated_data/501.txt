Environmental association Land van Cuijk will hold a theme evening about agriculture and nature in the context of the Participation Environment Act on April 20 at 7:30 PM in the De Jachthoorn community center in Sint Hubert. Geert Verstegen of the Environmental Association and HAS university lecturers Christ Tielemans and Ralph Köhne speak about modernizations in farming. More at mlvc.nl/Omgevingswet. Register at ambientwet@mlvc.nl, stating April 20.
