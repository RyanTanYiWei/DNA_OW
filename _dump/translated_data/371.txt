The three so-called Line 50 municipalities are currently working on a possible joint environmental vision. This is a regional implementation of the national Environmental Act, which should come into effect on January 1 next year. Would, indeed, because the introduction of the law has been postponed. The corona crisis is currently taking away a lot of preparation time.
This also applies to the three municipalities that just wanted to start using the Future Bus this spring. This bus would stop at various places and then collect ideas from citizens about their view of their living environment. This consultation round should be included again in this environmental vision. "Because meetings are not possible at the moment, we have had to decide in Line 50 to put the entire process on hold, so that everyone will soon be given the opportunity to participate," says councilor John Coenen of the municipality of Vaals. According to him, the following applies to this process: care before speed.
The municipalities are now waiting to see what the government's planning will look like for the introduction of the new law. According to the councilor, scenarios are being developed in the background so that the municipalities can start again when the green light is given.
