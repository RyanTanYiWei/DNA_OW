Environmental association Land van Cuijk will hold an information meeting about energy and climate on Wednesday, June 22 at 7:30 PM in the Jachthoorn in Sint Hubert. The central question is how the energy transition can take shape in the country of Cuijk. Presentations will be given by policy officials from the municipality of Land van Cuijk, Enexis and the Cuijks Energy Collective. You can register for the information evening by emailing ambientwet@mlvc.nl
