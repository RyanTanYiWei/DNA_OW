There will be an information meeting in Sint Hubert on Wednesday at 7:30 PM about environmental changes in the Land van Cuijk. Environmental association Land van Cuijk has invited various organizations, such as the Brabants Landschap and Waterschap Aa en Maas. There is talk of drought and the transition of agriculture. Location is De Jachthoorn in Sint Hubert. Register: ambientwet@mlvc.nl
