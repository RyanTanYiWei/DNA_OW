The 'Future Bus' that would collect ideas for tackling the Environmental Vision in Eijsden-Margraten, Gulpen-Wittem and Vaals next week will not be running for the time being. This is due to the measures surrounding the coronavirus. De Lijn 50 municipalities are working on a joint environmental vision in the context of the new Environmental Act. They want to know from residents how they see the future in terms of life, work, housing, recreation and traffic.
