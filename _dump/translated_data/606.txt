'Lighter and miracle taker' Engbert Breuker calls councilor Alexander Lurvink's plan 'a nice proposal at a micro level'. Henk Willem Tiktak speaks of a very nice idea, but sees the bottleneck that the swarm democracy runs through a political party. "After all, anyone who sits in a municipal council is not easily trusted." Strategist Ferenc van Damme appreciates Lurvink's idea, which reminds him of a hybrid car: half petrol, half electric. "The Environmental Act already makes citizens less dependent on governments." That makes Lurvink's proposal an intermediate step at most."
