Historical association Marithaime will give a lecture on March 21 entitled 'Heritage Act and Environmental Act; How do we deal with our Heritage?'.

The lecture is in Het Wapen van Elst and will be held by Bjorn van Snippenburg, member of Marithaime and landscape historian. The meeting starts at 9 p.m.

Admission is free for members and an entrance fee of 5 euros for non-members. This price includes a cup of coffee or tea. More information can be found on the website marithaime.nl.
