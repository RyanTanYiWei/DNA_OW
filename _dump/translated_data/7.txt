Farmers Defense Force (FDF) wants to take action on Tuesday and 'personally' deliver a cry for help to King Willem-Alexander. According to the farmers' action group, the king is their 'last and only hope' to protect farmers against the government's policy. It has not been announced exactly what the protest movement plans to do. FDF wants to take action against the government's purchase scheme and other nitrogen plans that are included in the Environmental Act. Agriculture Minister Carola Schouten wants her new nitrogen plans to be discussed by the House of Representatives before the Christmas holidays, much to the dismay of FDF. "Who can save us from the Environmental Act, which will have to be passed before Christmas if it is up to Rutte III?" the action group writes on its website. 'The Dutch Head of State. The ultimate Final Boss. His signature on the Environmental Act ultimately seals the fate of all Dutch farmers. Last month, FDF rang in the evening with a fruit basket at D66 faction leader Rob Jetten, who was in quarantine due to corona. Jetten was not charmed by this action.
