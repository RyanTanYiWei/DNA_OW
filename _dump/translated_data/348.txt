B15 ECONOMY
Minister Schultz van Haegen (Infrastructure and Environment) presented the Environment Act yesterday. According to her, construction will be faster and better under the new law.
One of the key points: after 2018, a would-be builder/renovator will only need one permit for a project, instead of dozens, as is sometimes the case now.
PvdA and SP yesterday made critical comments.
