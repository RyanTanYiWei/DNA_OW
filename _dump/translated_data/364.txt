doetinchem
The municipality of Doetinchem is competing for the Aandeslag Trophy 2019. This is intended for organizations with inspiring Environmental Act projects. The new environmental law will come into effect in 2021. This law should make the rules for the organization of the Netherlands easier. Doetinchem is one of the five nominees, who were chosen from 29 entries. According to the jury, Doetinchem is involving society well in the rollout of the new law.
