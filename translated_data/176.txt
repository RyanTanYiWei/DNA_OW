Amsterdam The administrative committee of the Amsterdam Center district wants residents to have a say again about the Holocaust Names Monument that should be placed in the Wertheim Park. The committee wants the central city council to reconsider the location. "The consultation procedure did not go well," SP faction leader Nelly Duijndam said. The VVD agrees with this. D66 and PvdA believe that the council should listen to the complaints of local residents.
        Faster construction through one law The Hague The many laws and regulations relating to spatial planning and the living environment are incorporated into one new law. This Environmental Act should make obtaining a permit faster and easier. Certain procedures can now take forever. If a school wants to expand in a residential area and it is necessary to cut down trees and create additional exits, three environmental permits are now required. That procedure takes six months. Soon it will be one permit and it will take eight weeks all in all.
