Dokkum Noardeast-Fryslân will draw up a vision about the future of Dokkum with residents and entrepreneurs. Such a vision is prescribed by the Environmental Act, which will come into effect next year. The process is supervised by consultancy firm Weusthuis and Partners from Groningen. The aim is to have drawn up a Dokkum Regional City Vision by the end of this year.
